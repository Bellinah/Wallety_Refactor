The power of #please pay for me

Sipho and his wife, Nombulelo live in Tembisa, where safety is a constant concern. The electricity suddenly goes out, plunging their small home into darkness.

Sipho’s, working the night shift. Nombulelo grabs her phone and opens Wallety, the convenient mobile payment app. With a few taps, she selects purchase electricity, enters the amount. She then clicks request a beneficiary to pay for you and proceeds to enter her husband’s number and clicks send. Within seconds Sipho get a message on Wallety requesting to pay. He clicks on pay and proceeds to pay the amount using his bank card. Within minutes the lights at home flicker back on.

“I’m blessed to have Wallety, ensuring Nombulelo’s safety and comfort. It’s reassuring to know I can provide for her, even from afar.” – Sipho
